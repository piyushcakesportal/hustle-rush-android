# Hustle Rush Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 23, 2026**

Hustle Rush is an offline mobile game. The game is designed to work without creating an account or sending personal information to the developer.

## Information collected

Hustle Rush does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, location, contacts, camera, microphone, or internet access.

## Information stored on your device

The game stores your highest score and gameplay preferences locally on your device. This information is used only to provide game functionality and is not transmitted to the developer.

## Children

The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

## Changes to this policy

If the game later adds advertising, analytics, online accounts, or other data-processing features, this policy and the Google Play Data safety declaration will be updated before release.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the email address shown on the Hustle Rush Google Play listing.
